About PawSome

Our mission is to rescue and provide a safe haven for street dogs and cats, offering them the care they deserve.

What We Do

At PawSome, we are dedicated to rescuing street animals in need, providing them with food, shelter, and medical care. Over the past five years, we have worked tirelessly to improve the lives of countless dogs and cats, giving them a second chance at life.

  • Rehabilitating and socializing the animals to make them adoptable
  • Finding loving, forever homes for all our rescued animals

Our Impact Over the Last 5 Years

Over the past five years, we have made a significant impact in the lives of street dogs and cats. Through the hard work of our team and the support of donors and volunteers, we have been able to:

  • Rescue over 1,000 street animals
  • Provide medical care for more than 500 injured animals
  • Successfully adopt out over 800 animals to loving families
  • Run multiple food and shelter campaigns during the monsoon season

We are committed to ensuring that no animal is left behind and that they get the chance they deserve for a better life.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who share a deep love for animals and are committed to the cause. From veterinarians to volunteers, we work together to ensure the safety and well-being of every animal in our care.

Whether it's providing medical treatment, finding foster homes, or spreading awareness about the plight of street animals, our team is always ready to step in and make a difference.

  • Make a donation to help fund medical care, food, and shelter
  • Volunteer with us to care for the animals
